Professional Lawn Care Services

Maintaining a healthy, vibrant lawn requires professional expertise and regular care. Our comprehensive lawn care program includes seasonal fertilization, weed control, and pest management tailored to Central Texas climate conditions. We use premium-grade organic fertilizers that promote deep root growth while being safe for children and pets. Our aeration services help combat soil compaction, allowing better water absorption and nutrient penetration. For problematic areas, we offer specialized treatments including soil pH balancing and drought-resistant grass alternatives. Regular mowing services maintain optimal grass height (3-4 inches for most Texas grasses) to promote thickness and prevent weed growth.

Landscape Design & Installation

Transform your outdoor space with our custom landscape design services. Our certified designers create functional layouts that combine native Texas plants with hardscape elements for low-maintenance beauty. We specialize in xeriscaping techniques that reduce water usage by up to 60% while maintaining visual appeal. Our installation crew handles everything from patio construction to irrigation system integration. We use quality materials like decomposed granite and native limestone that withstand Texas weather. Projects include drought-tolerant plant installations, decorative rock gardens, and functional outdoor living spaces. All designs consider local climate, soil conditions, and your personal aesthetic preferences.

Seasonal Garden Maintenance

Keep your garden thriving year-round with our seasonal maintenance programs. Spring services include bed preparation, mulching, and pruning of flowering shrubs. Summer focuses on irrigation optimization and pest management. Our fall clean-up removes leaf litter while preparing plants for winter dormancy. Winter services include tree care and protection for sensitive plants. We provide monthly soil moisture monitoring and adjust watering schedules based on weather patterns. Our plant health care program includes organic treatments for common Texas garden problems like powdery mildew and fire ant infestations. Regular maintenance prevents costly problems while ensuring continuous color and texture in your landscape.

Tree & Shrub Care

Proper arboriculture practices ensure the health and longevity of your landscape investments. Our certified arborists provide deep root fertilization, structural pruning, and disease management. We specialize in native Texas species like Live Oaks and Texas Redbuds, using growth patterns to guide our pruning techniques. Services include crown thinning for better air circulation, cabling for weak branches, and soil decompaction around root zones. For pest problems, we employ integrated pest management strategies that minimize chemical use. Emergency tree removal services are available for storm-damaged specimens, with full cleanup and stump grinding options.

Irrigation Solutions

Efficient water management is crucial in Texas landscapes. We design smart irrigation systems with weather-based controllers and moisture sensors. Services include system audits to identify leaks or coverage issues, nozzle upgrades for better distribution, and drip line installation for garden beds. Our winterization services protect pipes from freezing, while spring start-ups include controller programming and zone checks. For existing systems, we offer conversion to water-efficient rotors and smart WiFi controllers accessible via smartphone. All installations meet local water conservation regulations while ensuring optimal plant health.
